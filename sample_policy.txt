# CoverBots Auto Policy - Gold Plan

## 1. Usage
This policy covers private use only. Commercial use is strictly excluded.

## 2. Off-Road Driving
Off-road driving is covered ONLY if the vehicle is a 4x4 and the driver is over 25 years old.
If the driver is under 25, off-road driving is excluded.

## 3. Excess
A standard excess of BWP 5,000 applies to all claims.
